Management Molecule Dynamics™ is a uniqe systematic approach to get the whole organization to work towards the same goal in a structured & agile way

Management

The strategy towards a vision, build on common goals and a shared purpose.

Molecule

Visualizing the way of working, flexible and transparent, the molecule changes constantly and new teams are added or closed down.

Dynamics

The way of working builds on team skills and the collective intelligence, flat and non hierarchical, the ability to create high performing teams is the essence of the dynamics.

The Molecule = structure & cooperation

The molecule system optimize and streamline decision-making, ensuring cross-functional cooperation- a tool for creating a high performance organizational behavior to work together towards the same goal.

  • Increase involvement
  • Faster and better decisions
  • Better utilization of skills
  • Efficiency
  • Develop and secure the interfaces in the processes
  • Mutual responsibility in teams
  • All teams have a clear purpose, agenda, rules and KPI's (4P method)

All crossfunctional teams of the system are mutually responsible to get solutions to work.
